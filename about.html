Our Story

Born from a Passion for Excellence

Coconut Louge Lifestyle was born in Francistown from a simple but powerful belief that the people of Botswana deserve a world class lounge experience right at home. What started as a dream to create something different has grown into one of Francistown's most beloved and recognised lifestyle destinations.

What started as a dream has grown into one of Francistown's most loved and respected lifestyle destinations. We have hosted thousands of guests from intimate date nights to big celebrations.
